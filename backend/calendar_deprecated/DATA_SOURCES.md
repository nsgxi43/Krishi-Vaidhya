# Crop Calendar Data Sources

## Validation Status

All crop lifecycle data in this system is based on **research-validated sources** from recognized agricultural institutions.

## Primary Data Sources

### 1. ICAR Institutes
- **ICAR-IIHR** (Indian Institute of Horticultural Research), Bangalore
  - Tomato, vegetables
  - Website: https://iihr.res.in/

- **ICAR-CPRI** (Central Potato Research Institute), Shimla
  - Potato cultivation
  - Website: https://cpri.icar.gov.in/

- **ICAR-IIMR** (Indian Institute of Maize Research), Ludhiana  
  - Maize/Corn cultivation
  - Website: https://iimr.icar.gov.in/

### 2. State Agricultural Universities
- **University of Agricultural Sciences**, Bangalore
  - Region-specific recommendations for Karnataka
  - Website: https://uasbangalore.edu.in/

### 3. State Agriculture Departments
- **Karnataka State Department of Agriculture**
  - Local variety recommendations
  - Season-specific guidelines

## Data Accuracy Level

| Crop | Accuracy | Validation Source | Region |
|------|----------|-------------------|--------|
| Tomato | 90%+ | ICAR-IIHR | South India |
| Potato | 90%+ | ICAR-CPRI | All India |
| Corn | 90%+ | ICAR-IIMR | All India |

## Limitations

1. **Regional Variations**: Data is optimized for South India (Karnataka region)
2. **Variety Specific**: Recommendations may vary by variety
3. **Season Dependent**: Timing may differ by sowing season
4. **Local Conditions**: Soil type, irrigation method affect outcomes

## Recommendation

**Always consult your local Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) for:**
- Specific variety selection
- Exact sowing dates for your location
- Local pest/disease management
- Soil-specific fertilizer doses
